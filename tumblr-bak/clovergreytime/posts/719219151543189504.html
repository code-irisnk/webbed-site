Take a breath, now.


Take another.


Feel air in your lungs.


Let your limbs return.




Yes,

move your fingers.




Have a body again,

under gravity,

in air.





Respawn in the long dream.




There you are.

Your body touching the universe again at every point,

as though you were separate things.



As though we were separate things.
